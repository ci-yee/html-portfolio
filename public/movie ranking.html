My Top Three Movies

I think...

Come On, Come On

Watched it winter 2021, on a solo movie date at Tivoli just before Christmas holiday.
I remember crying a lot by myself sitting at the back row of the cinema every 20 minutes of the film. It was a simple, heart-warming film shot in black and white. By far one of the best films I've watched. No fancy sfx or massive budgets, just a humble film that touches your heart through story-telling.

Everything, everywhere, all at once

Not much to say about this film other than just pure chaotic energy. At multiple points of the film I thought this could easily be such a trash film but they've somehow made it so so good, it was simply iconic.

Your Name

Was my favourite film for a long time until the whole time-travelling loop hole started bothering me. Nonetheless, it was a film that made me extremely delulu and I still beleive in parallel multiverse and all that jazz.
